About Me

I'm a nutrition coach who helps people build sustainable habits without guilt or extremes.

After pregnancy, I struggled with body changes and pressure to "bounce back." That's when I realized: people don't need more diets — they need support and real solutions.

I trained with Mac-Nutrition Uni (MNU), a leading evidence-based program, and now work with clients of all backgrounds — from athletes to those managing PCOS or type 2 diabetes.

I won't offer quick fixes. I'll help you understand your body, eat with confidence, and build habits that fit your real life.

FAQ

Questions?
I've got answers.

Still on the fence? Here are the most common questions I get before we start.

Still have questions?
Do I need to follow a strict meal plan?
+
No. I don't believe in rigid meal plans. Instead, we build a flexible framework that fits your real life — including social dinners, travel, and the occasional glass of wine.
I'm over 45 — is nutrition coaching still effective?
+
Absolutely — this is exactly when nutrition becomes most powerful. Hormonal shifts, metabolism changes, and energy fluctuations after 40 can all be addressed with the right strategy. My programs are specifically designed for this life stage.
How does online coaching work?
+
Everything is done via video calls (Zoom or Google Meet) and a messaging platform. You get access to a private client portal where all your materials, check-ins, and progress tracking live. Timezone differences are no problem — I work with clients worldwide.
How quickly will I see results?
+
Most clients notice improvements in energy and digestion within the first 2–3 weeks. Visible body changes typically follow within 6–8 weeks. The 3-month program is designed to create lasting results — not quick fixes that fade.
What if I have dietary restrictions or health conditions?
+
I always take your full health history into account. Whether you're gluten-free, vegetarian, have thyroid issues, or anything else — your plan is customized to your unique situation. I always recommend working alongside your doctor for medical conditions.
What's the investment for the 3-month program?
+
The 3-month program starts from $450, depending on your goals and the level of support needed. Payment plans are available. The best way to get an accurate quote is to book a free discovery call — no obligation, just a conversation.
