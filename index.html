FAQ

Questions? I've got answers.

Still on the fence? Here are the most common questions I get before we start working together.

Still have questions?
Do I need to follow a strict meal plan?
+
No. I don't believe in rigid meal plans that make you feel like you're on a diet. Instead, we build a flexible framework that fits your real life — including social dinners, travel, and the occasional glass of wine.
I'm over 45 — is nutrition coaching still effective for me?
+
Absolutely — in fact, this is exactly when nutrition becomes most powerful. Hormonal shifts, metabolism changes, and energy fluctuations after 40 can all be addressed with the right nutritional strategy. My programs are specifically designed for this life stage.
How does online coaching work?
+
Everything is done via video calls (Zoom or Google Meet) and a messaging platform. You get access to a private client portal where all your materials, check-ins, and progress tracking live. Timezone differences are no problem — I work with clients worldwide.
How quickly will I see results?
+
Most clients notice improvements in energy and digestion within the first 2–3 weeks. Visible body changes typically follow within 6–8 weeks. The 3-month program is designed to create lasting results — not quick fixes that fade.
What if I have dietary restrictions or health conditions?
+
I always take your full health history into account. Whether you're gluten-free, vegetarian, have thyroid issues, or anything else — your plan is customized to your unique situation. I always recommend working alongside your doctor for medical conditions.
What's the investment for the 3-month program?
+
The 3-month program starts from $450, depending on your goals and the level of support needed. Payment plans are available. The best way to get an accurate quote is to book a free discovery call — there's no obligation, just a conversation.
